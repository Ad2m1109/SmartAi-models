SmartAI – 8 AI Features at Launch of "Allt i Allo"
1. Autofill via Image Recognition (Free)
What it does:
 The user uploads a photo → AI automatically fills in the title, brand, color, and category.
Example:
 A photo of a power drill → “Bosch Blue Cordless Drill, 18V – Tools”
Why it matters:
 Creates a strong "wow-effect" at first use. Reduces friction for new users and automatically improves listing quality — especially useful for mobile users in a hurry.

2. SmartAI Ad Generator
What it does:
 Provides automatic suggestions for headlines, descriptions, categories, and pricing — either step-by-step or fully automated.
Example:
 You want to rent out a hammer drill → AI generates a complete listing.
Why it matters:
 Makes it easy for everyone to create good listings, even if they don’t know how. Lowers the entry barrier for beginners and saves time for experienced users. A key feature for rapid platform growth.

3. SmartAI Real-Time Pricing
What it does:
 Suggests prices based on demand, location, category, and historical data.
Example:
 “Set the price to 120 SEK/day – high demand in your area.”
Why it matters:
 Helps users earn more and get more bookings. Builds trust in the platform and signals data-driven and market-intelligent operations.

4. SmartAI Demand Forecast
What it does:
 Shows how demand for a product or service is expected to change in the coming days.
Example:
 “Inflatable kids’ pools will get 3x more clicks next week due to a heatwave.”
Why it matters:
 Gives users strategic insights and reinforces the idea that Allt i Allo is “in the know.” Encourages return usage and boosts retention.

5. SmartAI Listing Comparison
What it does:
 Compares a user’s ad to similar ones in terms of length, price, photos, etc.
Example:
 “Your description is 30% shorter than average – consider adding more details.”
Why it matters:
 Provides concrete and actionable feedback to improve listings. Enhances user success and reinforces the feeling that the platform cares about their performance.

6. SmartAI Text Optimizer ("CopyCoach") – For Service Providers and Clients
What it does:
 Improves service-related texts — whether you're offering a service or looking for one.
Examples:
Provider: “I can help you move” → “Reliable moving help with van & lifting assistance – Stockholm”


Client: “Need help painting” → “Looking for a precise painter for 25 sqm living room – preferably weekend, Stockholm”


Why it matters:
 A unique feature that strengthens both sides of the service market. Encourages clearer communication, builds trust, and increases conversion rates — especially valuable as the service module grows.

7. SmartAI Earnings Simulator ("Boost Simulator")
What it does:
 Shows potential earnings based on user activity, location, and demand.
Example:
 “If you rent out 3 times/month → You earn 2,400 SEK net/month”
Why it matters:
 Motivates users to be more active and use the platform strategically. Reinforces the idea that Allt i Allo is an income opportunity — ideal for private users and micro-entrepreneurs.

8. AI-Powered Publishing Time Optimizer
What it does:
 Suggests the best time and day to publish a listing for maximum visibility.
Example:
 “Post on Monday at 07:00 – highest chance to reach the top list.”
Why it matters:
 Simple feature with immediate impact. Helps users maximize exposure and shows that Allt i Allo understands algorithms and trends — enhancing perceived professionalism.
